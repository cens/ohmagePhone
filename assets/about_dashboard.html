The Dashboard makes it easy to get to campaigns, surveys, and your response history

Campaigns

On the Campaign List screen you can see the campaigns you have downloaded to the phone as well as add new campaigns. You can also access information about the campaigns you have downloaded as well as set up triggers.

Surveys

On the Survey List screen you can see and take surveys from all of the campaigns that have been downloaded to the phone.

Response History

On the Response History screen you can see calendar and map views of your response history.

Upload Queue

In the Upload Queue you can manage the upload status of the responses you have just submitted.

Profile

On the Profile screen you can update your password, or logout and wipe your data.
